# PRODUCT.md — Valuatum AI-arvonmääritysraportti

## What this is

A Finnish-language, conversion-focused landing page selling AI-assisted company
valuation reports (PDF) for Finnish companies. MVP goal: sell reports next week.

## Brand & tone

- Brand: Valuatum Oy — 25+ years of valuation, credit risk and equity research
  systems for banks and research houses. Sister product: aiequityreports.com.
- Tone: professional B2B finance, accessible but credible. No "AI magic" hype,
  no retail-consumer feel, no aggressive competitor claims.
- Honesty is a feature: automatic generation is NOT live yet. All copy says
  reports are delivered by email; browser generation "tulossa myöhemmin".

## Users

1. Entrepreneurs considering selling their company
2. Buyers evaluating a target
3. Accountants / consultants needing valuation support
4. Lawyers (M&A, disputes, inheritance, divorce, ownership changes)
5. Investors and board members
6. Companies preparing for funding or restructuring

## Strategic principles

- Conversion over SEO architecture; one page, strong CTAs to #tilaa and #esimerkit
- Show quality before purchase (sample reports)
- Differentiate on explanation: methods used AND rejected, decisive assumptions,
  risks, data limitations, value-improvement actions — not a black-box number
- Legally careful: never claim fairness opinion / audit / legal statement
- Don't look cheap: 79 € launch price framed as B2B expert analysis
